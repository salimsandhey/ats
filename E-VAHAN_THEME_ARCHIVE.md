# eVAHAN Color Mapping Archive (Previous Themes)

This document records exactly which colors were used and where they were applied in the previous professional iterations of the eVAHAN landing page.

---

## 🟠 02. The Signal Industrial Theme (Last Used)

**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** `#FF3E00` (Signal Orange)
- **Ink:** `#121212` (Deep Charcoal)
- **Porcelain:** `#F8F8F8` (Off-White)
- **Stone:** `#F2F2F2` (Soft Gray)
- **Silver:** `#E2E2E2` (Divider Gray)

**Usage Mapping:**
- **#FF3E00 (Primary):** Used for "Enquire Now" pulse, Section headers, Icon bullets, Contact form "eV" watermark, and CTA button hover states.
- **#121212 (Ink):** Used for all primary headlines, main paragraph text, navigation links, and dark-themed section backgrounds.
- **#F8F8F8 (Porcelain):** Used as the primary canvas background for the Hero, Setup Support, and Intelligence sections.
- **#F2F2F2 (Stone):** Used as the secondary background for the About, Regulatory, and Engineering sections to create visual depth.
- **#E2E2E2 (Silver):** Used for all structural borders, horizontal dividers, and secondary component outlines.
